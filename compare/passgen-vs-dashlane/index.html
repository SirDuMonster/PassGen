Detailed Analysis

Cost & Value

PassGen is completely free with all features available. Dashlane's free tier is extremely limited (25 passwords on 1 device). To get useful functionality, you need Premium at $60/year or Family at $90/year—among the most expensive password managers available.

Winner: PassGen - Free vs $60/year for basic functionality

Password Generation

PassGen offers superior generation options: passphrases, PINs, usernames, memorable passwords, and bulk generation up to 100 at once. Dashlane's generator is basic—random passwords only with limited customization.

Winner: PassGen - More generation types and options

Extra Features

Dashlane Premium includes a VPN and dark web monitoring for leaked credentials. These are valuable additions if you don't already have them, but they add to the cost and aren't related to password generation.

Winner: Dashlane - VPN and monitoring are nice extras (if you need them)

Accessibility

PassGen works instantly—visit the site and generate. Dashlane requires account creation, app installation, and even then the free tier is nearly unusable. For occasional password generation, PassGen wins decisively.

Winner: PassGen - Zero friction, instant access
