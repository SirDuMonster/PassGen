Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

The Short Version

We don't collect your passwords or personal data. All password generation happens in your browser. We can't see, store, or access the passwords you generate.

1. Information We Don't Collect

PassGen is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect:

  • Passwords you generate
  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Account credentials of any kind
  • Browsing history or behavior data
  • IP addresses for tracking purposes

2. How Password Generation Works

All password generation occurs entirely within your web browser using the Web Cryptography API. When you generate a password:

  • The password is created locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • We have no way to see or recover generated passwords
  • The site can function completely offline once loaded

3. Local Storage

PassGen may use your browser's local storage to remember:

  • Your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Your preferred settings (password length, character types)

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

4. Hosting and Infrastructure

PassGen is hosted on Vercel. While Vercel may collect standard web server logs (which may include IP addresses and access times for security and performance purposes), we do not access or use this data to track individual users.

5. Third-Party Services

PassGen does not use:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Third-party cookies

6. Cookies

PassGen does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. The only cookies that may be present are:

  • Essential cookies required by our hosting provider for security
  • Your theme preference (stored as a simple local preference)

7. Children's Privacy

PassGen does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Our service is a simple tool that requires no account creation or personal information submission.

8. Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to secure on our end. Your generated passwords exist only in your browser until you close the page or copy them elsewhere.

9. Your Rights

Because we don't collect personal data, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) don't apply. You maintain complete control over any passwords you generate—we never have access to them.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Significant changes will be noted on our homepage.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our contact page.
