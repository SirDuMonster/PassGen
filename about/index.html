About PassGen

Free, secure password generation for everyone

Our Mission

PassGen exists to make strong password security accessible to everyone—free, instant, and private. We believe that creating secure passwords shouldn't require subscriptions, accounts, or technical expertise.

Why We Built PassGen

Weak passwords remain one of the leading causes of account breaches. Yet many password generators are buried inside paid password managers, require account creation, or lack the advanced features users need.

We created PassGen to solve these problems:

  • No barriers - Generate passwords instantly, no signup required
  • Complete privacy - Everything runs in your browser, nothing is sent to servers
  • Comprehensive tools - Passwords, passphrases, PINs, usernames, and more
  • Education - Understand why your passwords are strong or weak

How PassGen Works

PassGen uses the Web Cryptography API—the same cryptographic standard used by banks and security professionals—to generate truly random passwords. All generation happens client-side in your browser.

100% Client-Side

Your passwords are generated entirely in your browser. They never touch our servers because they never leave your device.

No Tracking

We don't track what passwords you generate, how often you visit, or any personal information.

Works Offline

Once loaded, PassGen works without an internet connection. Perfect for air-gapped security.

Our Tools

  • Password Generator - Customizable random passwords
  • Passphrase Generator - Word-based memorable passphrases
  • Memorable Passwords - Easy to remember, hard to crack
  • PIN Generator - Secure numeric PINs
  • WiFi Password Generator - Router-compatible passwords
  • Username Generator - Unique usernames for any platform
  • Bulk Generator - Generate up to 100 passwords at once
  • Strength Checker - Analyze any password's security

Open & Transparent

PassGen is built with standard web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) that anyone can inspect. Right-click, view source, and see exactly how your passwords are generated. No hidden code, no obfuscation.
